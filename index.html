About Us

With the constant evolution of technology, the Internet, and it's integration into society, we believe that the information and practicality of such tools should be available to all. Our goal is to help bridge the gap for those who may experience issues with accessibility. This is why we formed Accessible Design Co.

We're founded on the principle of universal design and accessibility-first development. We seek to achieve this through several vectors such as raising awareness among future developers, teaching/training in good html practices that promote accessibility, features such as aria, and through our own browser extensions to assist with accessibility.

We're passionate about breaking down digital barriers and empowering organizations to embrace accessibility not just as a requirement, but as a foundation for better user experiences for all.
